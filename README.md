# RacingGame_Raylib

Your goal is to control the two cars and cross the finish line as fast as possible with both of them. 

### Game Screenshot
![image](https://github.com/Oreyato/RacingGame_Raylib/blob/main/README/RG_InGame.png)

### Controls
QD: rotate red car | ZS: move red car | spacebar: drift red car

right & left arrows: rotate blue car | top & down arrows: move blue car | 0: drift blue car
